Rate My City

During my time at Actualize I created many apps. One of the applications I was most proud of was called Rate My City. This app really helped me to grasp as a user what information people want to see. The purpose and inspiration for my app was due to my life experience dealing with the northern california wildfires.

Personally built an app which allows users to create a login and review a city that they live in. Then users can compare and review overall ratings for all other cities in the database. Built on a Ruby-on-Rails backend and a Vue.js frontend, incorporating and modifying Bootstrap elements and JavaScript transition elements.

One of the things I kept saying to myself when I was working on this project was "what information would I want to see on this page?". With this thought process I was able to deliver clear and concise information that any user could find valuable.
